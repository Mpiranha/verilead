    Create Custom Domain
    Set up your domain to connect with verileads for website.
    1. Login to your account you have with your domain provider.
    2. Find the DNS settings. Look for your account admin panel or your domain management area, or DNS configuration area or simlar.
    3. Edit your A record to point to verilead's IP address
      1. The admin panel might feature drop-downs or editable fields or you might have to check boxes or edit a tale in the admin you need.
      2. Enter or choose the @ symbol or A record.
      3. Enter Verilead's IP address as the destination for the A record.
    4. Save the A record (click save, save zone file, add record or similar).
    5. Find the CNAME record in your DNS settings.
    6. Change your www CNAME record to point to app.verileads.co.
    7. Save the CNAME record (Usually by clicking Save or Add record or similar).
    8. Your website URL (Example). Website URL:192.64.119.72. 192.64.119.72 https://www.example.com or https://www.example.com
